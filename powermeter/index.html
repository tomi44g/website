Wattmeter in your pocket!

PowerMeter App turns your iPhone/iPod Touch into a Wattmeter. Pointing the camera at the red flashing LED of your electricity meter you can get the reading in seconds! Without any additional hardware you can easily find out how much energy is your TV set using in a standby mode or check how much your solar PV panels produce. Download the App now and start saving money!

Features

Measure Power

Cut your electricity costs and your carbon footprint by up to 10%. No need to buy any additional piece of hardware.

Current Transformer

As an In-App purchase you can unlock Current Transformer feature. You will not need to multiply the reading by CT ratio any more.

Change Title

Your measurements are saved for further reference. You can assign a meaningful title to every measurement to stay organised.

Check Stats

Check what was the average, minimum and maximum power consumption during the survey.

Used by professionals

PowerMeter gives reliable readings and it is being used by professional electricians all around the word.
